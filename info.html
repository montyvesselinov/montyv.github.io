I earned MEng degree at the University of Mining and Geology, Sofia, Bulgaria, in 1989, and PhD degree at the University of Arizona, Tucson in 2000.

I worked as a Tenured Assistant Professor at the Department of Hydrogeology and Engineering Geology, University of Mining and Geology, Sofia, Bulgaria.

Presently, I am a technical staff member at the Computation Earth Science Group, Earth and Environmental Sciences Division, Los Alamos National Laboratory, New Mexico.

My research interests include numerical modeling of flow and transport in variably saturated media, stochastic inverse methods for characterization of medium heterogeneity (tomography), scale effects and Levy stable distributions, fractal and multi-fractal techniques, high-performance computing (parallelization), decision support and risk assessment.
